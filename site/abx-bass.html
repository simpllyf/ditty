ABX — can you actually hear the bass change?

A = today's bass (cold jumps). B = leads into changes. X is secretly A or B, re-rolled each trial. Listen to all three (same track, same moment — switch freely), then say which one X is. After 12 trials the maths decides: reliably right → audible; near chance → your ear can't tell them apart.
